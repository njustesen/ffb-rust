# Grak and Crumbleberry

**250K**

### Grak

MA | ST | AG | PA | AV  
---|---|---|---|---  
5 | 5 | 4+ | 4+ | 10+  
  
_(Big Guy, Ogre)_

  * [Bone Head](../../core_rules/skills_and_traits/#bone-head-passive)
  * [Kick Team-mate](../../core_rules/skills_and_traits/#kick-team-mate-passive)
  * [Loner](../../core_rules/skills_and_traits/#loner-x-passive) (4+)
  * [Mighty Blow](../../core_rules/skills_and_traits/#mighty-blow-active)
  * [Thick Skull](../../core_rules/skills_and_traits/#thick-skull-passive)
  * **I'll Carry You**

Grak & Crumbleberry must be hired as a pair. Additionally, once per half, if Grak begins his activation adjacent to Crumbleberry he may pick up Crumbleberry; temporarily remove Crumbleberry from the pitch. At the end of Grak's activation, place Crumbleberry in an unoccupied square adjacent to Grak.

### Crumbleberry

MA | ST | AG | PA | AV  
---|---|---|---|---  
5 | 2 | 3+ | 5+ | 7+  
  
_(Halfling, Lineman)_

  * [Dodge](../../core_rules/skills_and_traits/#dodge-active)
  * [Lethal Flight](../../core_rules/skills_and_traits/#lethal-flight-active)
  * [Loner](../../core_rules/skills_and_traits/#loner-x-passive) (4+)
  * [Right Stuff](../../core_rules/skills_and_traits/#right-stuff-passive)
  * [Stunty](../../core_rules/skills_and_traits/#stunty-passive)
  * [Sure Hands](../../core_rules/skills_and_traits/#sure-hands-active)
  * **I'll Carry You**

Grak & Crumbleberry must be hired as a pair. Additionally, while Crumbleberry is being carried by Grak, Grak gains the Break Tackle and Dodge Skills.
