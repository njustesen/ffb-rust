Once per game, when Grombrindal is activated he may select one team-mate within 2 squares. The selected team-mate gains one of the following Skills until the end of turn: [Break Tackle](../../core_rules/skills_and_traits/#break-tackle-active), [Dauntless](../../core_rules/skills_and_traits/#dauntless-active), [Mighty Blow](../../core_rules/skills_and_traits/#mighty-blow-active), [Sure Feet](../../core_rules/skills_and_traits/#sure-feet-active).
